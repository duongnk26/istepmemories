🔰 For beginning runners, your mileage can increase by 20-25% each week. If you’re running 4-6km on your long run, you can safely add 1,5km or more each week to that run.

🔰 For more experienced runners, aim to increase by no more than 10-15% each week. This is especially important when your long run distance > 15km, as it helps prevent injuries and also gives you more time for personal life.

⚡️ Rest: Avoid using the knee.
⚡️ Ice: Apply a covered ice pack to the knee for 20 minutes/time, 4-8 times/ day.
⚡️ Compression: Wrap the knee in an elastic bandage to reduce swelling. Don't wrap it too tight, as it can interfere with blood circulation.
